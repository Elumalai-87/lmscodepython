Mechanics Questions

1. What is the velocity of an object that travels 100 meters in 5 seconds?

Answer: Velocity = distance/time = 100m / 5s = 20 m/s

2. Calculate the acceleration of a car that goes from rest to 20 m/s in 10 seconds.

Answer: Acceleration = change in velocity/time = (20 - 0) / 10 = 2 m/s²

Electromagnetism Questions

1. What is the force between two charges of 3 μC and 5 μC separated by a distance of 2 meters?

Answer: F = k * (q₁ * q₂) / r² = 8.99 * 10⁹ * (3 * 10⁻⁶ * 5 * 10⁻⁶) / (2²) = 0.337 N

2. Calculate the magnetic field strength at a distance of 1 meter from a long straight wire carrying a current of 5 A.

Answer: B = (μ₀ * I) / (2 * π * r) = (4π * 10⁻⁷ * 5) / (2 * π * 1) = 10⁻⁷ T

Thermodynamics Questions

1. What is the work done when a gas expands from 2 L to 5 L at constant pressure of 10 atm?

Answer: Work = pressure * change in volume = 10 atm * (5 - 2) L = 30 L·atm

2. Calculate the efficiency of a heat engine that absorbs 400 J of heat and does 200 J of work.

Answer: Efficiency = Work done / Heat absorbed = 200 / 400 = 0.5 or 50%
