Chemical Reactions Questions

1. What is the balanced equation for the combustion of methane (CH₄)?

Answer: CH₄ + 2O₂ → CO₂ + 2H₂O

2. What is the pH of a solution with a hydrogen ion concentration of 1 × 10⁻⁷ M?

Answer: pH = -log[H⁺] = -log(1 × 10⁻⁷) = 7 (Neutral)

Organic Chemistry Questions

1. What is the structure of methane (CH₄)?

Answer: Methane (CH₄) has a tetrahedral structure with the carbon atom at the center and the four hydrogen atoms at the vertices.

2. What is the difference between an alkane and an alkene?

Answer: Alkanes are saturated hydrocarbons with single bonds (C–C), while alkenes are unsaturated hydrocarbons with at least one double bond (C=C).

Periodic Table Questions

1. What is the atomic number of Oxygen?

Answer: Oxygen (O) has an atomic number of 8.

2. What element has the symbol 'Na'?

Answer: Sodium (Na) is the element with the symbol 'Na'.
