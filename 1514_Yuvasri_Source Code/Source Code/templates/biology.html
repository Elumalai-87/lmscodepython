Cell Biology Questions

1. What is the function of mitochondria in a cell?

Answer: Mitochondria are responsible for producing energy in the form of ATP through cellular respiration.

2. What is the difference between prokaryotic and eukaryotic cells?

Answer: Prokaryotic cells lack a nucleus and membrane-bound organelles, while eukaryotic cells have a defined nucleus and organelles.

Genetics Questions

1. What is a gene?

Answer: A gene is a segment of DNA that carries the instructions for synthesizing proteins, which determine an organism's traits.

2. What is the difference between dominant and recessive alleles?

Answer: A dominant allele expresses its trait over a recessive allele. A recessive allele requires two copies to express its trait.

Ecology Questions

1. What is an ecosystem?

Answer: An ecosystem is a community of living organisms interacting with each other and their physical environment.

2. What is the role of producers in an ecosystem?

Answer: Producers, such as plants, use sunlight to produce food through photosynthesis, providing energy for other organisms in the ecosystem.
